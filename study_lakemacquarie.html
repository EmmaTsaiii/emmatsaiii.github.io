A Night in Lake Macquarie

Reflect about your past, present and future.

Overview

We are honored to partner with Lake Macquarie City Council to design the interactive installations for their high-tech MAP / Mima precinct, a multi-purpose facility incorporated with high technology. In the notion of promoting community engagement and ultimately obtain economic benefits, A Night in Lake Macquarie integrates the building, art, environment and interactive elements into this innovative gathering space. It tells stories related to the environment and communities of Lake Macquarie region of NSW using digital and multimedia approaches for the digital interactive interfaces placemaking.

Background Research

The Lake Macquarie area encapsulates a variety of visually stunning natural features. Many people leave their homes in the city, venturing to Lake Macquarie to immerse themselves in a peaceful landscape surrounded by Australian bush and water views as far as the eye can see. While the vast waterscape of Lake Macquarie may be the most obvious natural beauty, the night sky is undoubtedly one of the most stunning natural features in Lake Macquarie.

Lake Macquarie is equally as defined by the people who live there-their stories, diversity and varying cultures. The night sky has long been a significant element in many if not all cultures globally, and is a shared symbol and reminder of our collective humanity. People have recorded and studied the astronomical phenomena for many thousands of years to explian its relations with people and nature.

Problem Statement

How might we create a safe, entertaining, and engaging night-time activity that enriches the everyday life of Lake Macquarie community?



Ideate

After collating the initial research, we aimed to pump out at least 4 ideas per group member for all the interfaces. These initial ideas were part of a brainstorming activity to generate innovative concepts. We analyzed the pros & cons, conducted brainwriting and sought external feedback via informal interviews and surveys from external sources to validate our designs. With three rounds of iterations and a discussion with the client, A Night in Lake Macquarie was born.

With particular emphasis on story-telling, A Night in Lake Macquarie, intergrated the LED catenary light and the Node wall, is a reflective and thought provoking interactive design that completes the environment and is capable of inspiring and providing users a fun, safe and immersive experience to make memories and share stories.
